Compare & Contrast Essay

Master the Art of Analytical Comparison

💼 Starting Your Own Business vs. Joining a Company

Starting a Business

  • 1 Be your own boss
  • 2 Control schedule
  • 3 Financial risk
  • 4 No guaranteed pay
  • 5 Choose direction

Both

  • A Need dedication
  • B Work with people
  • C Long hours
  • D Need skills
  • E Career growth

Joining a Company

  • 1 Steady income
  • 2 Benefits package
  • 3 Team support
  • 4 Follow directions
  • 5 Fixed schedule

🎯 Advanced Technique: Extended Metaphor

Extended Metaphor: Take your metaphor beyond one sentence and weave it throughout your essay!

Example: If you compare career choice to "sailing a ship"...

  • Introduction: "...sailing your own ship vs. boarding a cruise liner"
  • Body 1: "Entrepreneurs must navigate stormy markets..."
  • Body 2: "Company employees have an experienced captain at the helm..."
  • Conclusion: "Whether you chart your own course or trust another's navigation..."

💡 Challenge: Create an extended metaphor that appears in all 4 paragraphs!

📚 Evidence Bank (Review Before Writing)

Starting a Business

  • Financial potential - possibility of significant wealth
  • Being your own boss
  • No guaranteed stable income
  • High failure risk
  • Over 30 million entrepreneurs in the U.S.
  • Having control over your schedule
  • May have periods with no income at all

Joining a Company

  • Regular salaries
  • Guaranteed stable income
  • Set schedules and vacation time
  • Less personal financial risk
  • Team support and resources
  • Company benefits and structure

Both

  • Need "extreme dedication"
  • Require people skills - "put yourself in your customer's shoes"
  • Involve "managing deadlines"
  • "A company is simply a group of people"
  • Offer career growth opportunities
  • Require learning from mistakes

💡 Vocabulary Connection Examples

  • • The demanding nature of both career paths
  • • Critical decisions about your future
  • • Circumstances that influence your choice
  • • Jobs that contain different responsibilities
  • • Ways to boost your income
  • • Having conviction in your choice

✍️ Task 1: Introduction (3 minutes)

Write a Thesis Statement

Formula: While both _______ and _______ share _______ and _______, they differ in _______ and _______.

Transition Words to Consider:

  • Similarities: both, similarly, likewise, also
  • Differences: however, whereas, while, in contrast

Teacher Example (bare bones):
"While both paths involve _______, they differ in _______."

Reminder: Look at the evidence bank to choose your strongest points!

📝 Task 2: Body Paragraph 1 - Starting a Business (4 minutes)

Structure:

  1. Topic Sentence: Focus on entrepreneurship with both similarities and differences
  2. Similarity: What it shares with joining a company
  3. Difference: What makes it unique
  4. Closing Sentence: Wrap up the paragraph

Transition Starters:

  • • Topic: "Starting your own business..."
  • • Similarity: "Like employees in companies..."
  • • Difference: "However..." / "Unlike..." / "In contrast..."
  • • Closing: "Therefore..." / "Thus..."
