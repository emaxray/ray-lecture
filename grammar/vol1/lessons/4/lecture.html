❌ NO - It's incorrect!

Reason: "Student" is a singular countable noun.
Singular countable nouns MUST have an article!

✅ Correct: "Look at a student wearing a shirt"

The Golden Rule

📝 NOTES!

GOLDEN RULE:

Articles are ALWAYS used with
SINGULAR COUNTABLE NOUNS!

They're best friends - they stick together! 👫

❌ "Look at student" (WRONG - missing article)
✅ "Look at a student" (CORRECT)
✅ "Look at the student" (CORRECT)

It's SAFER to use an article than to skip it!

✨ The Answer:

"a" = ANY student (we don't know which one)
"the" = SPECIFIC student (we all know which one)

Rule: Use "the" ONLY when everyone knows what you're talking about!

The Decision Tree

Singular Countable Noun
Is it ONE? ✓
Do we know which one?
YES → Use "the"
(like a pronoun)
NO → Use "a/an"
(random/new)

Remember: When in doubt, put an article in!
Better to use one than to skip it! 🛡️

Definite Article (THE) - Challenge 1

🤔 Explain the Difference:

"I can see ___ moon" vs "It looks like ___ moon"

1
2

📝 NOTES! - Rule ①

✨ The Answer:

"I can see THE moon" (real moon - only one!)
"It looks like A moon" (moon-shaped thing)

Rule ①: Use "the" for unique things everyone knows!
Like the sun, the earth, the sky 🌍☀️

THE = Like a Pronoun!

📝 NOTES! - Rules ②③④⑤

"THE" works like a pronoun - everyone must know what you mean!

Examples:

Rule ②: "There is a red car. The car is mine."
= "There is a red car. It is mine." ✓

Rule ③: "Close the door!" (pointing)
= "Close it!" (pointing) ✓

Rule ④: "She plays the piano."
"He is the best." (the only one)

Rule ⑤: "The young need help."
= "They need help." (young people) ✓

✨ The Answer:

Rule ③: A/AN = "어떤" (a certain) - someone unknown
A/AN = ONE + RANDOM/UNKNOWN

A/AN - Two Simple Rules

📝 NOTES! - Rules ①②③④

Remember These Two Rules!

Rule 1

Always ONE thing
1️⃣

Rule 2

Random or Nobody knows
❓

All uses follow these rules:

① "Rome was not built in a day" (ONE day, ANY day)
② "Five times a day" (per ONE day unit - ~마다)
③ "A strange man" (ONE man, DON'T KNOW which - 어떤)
④ "A cat is clever" (ONE cat, ANY random cat - 대표 단수)

A or AN? Use Korean Sounds!

📝 NOTES! - Section 03

The Korean Sound Method

Is the first sound 아/애/에/이/오/우/어? → Use AN
Consonant sound? → Use A

AN (vowel sounds)

an apple (애플)
an umbrella (엄브렐라)
an hour (아워)
an uncle (엉클)

A (consonant sounds)

① a university (유니버시티)
① a European (유러피언)
① a uniform (유니폼)
a one-way (원웨이)

💡 Don't look at spelling! Listen to the Korean sound!
유니버시티 starts with ㅠ sound (not ㅜ) → A university

Special Compound Items

📝 NOTES! - Section 03 ②

Remember This Story:

"Needle and Thread were eating Bread and Butter,
using Fork and Knife, riding a Horse and Carriage"

🪡🧵 → 🍞🧈 → 🍴 → 🐴🚃

These pairs are such good PARTNERS, they share one article!

Test: Are they PARTNERS or STRANGERS?
• Knife and fork? → PARTNERS! (a knife and fork)
• Boy and girl? → STRANGERS! (a boy and a girl)
