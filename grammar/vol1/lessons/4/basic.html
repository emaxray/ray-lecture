üìñ Extra Study for Odd Numbers Students

7

‚ö†Ô∏è Why tricky? Students often write "has" because they think "the poor" is singular!

üéØ Advanced Analysis:

1) THE + adjective = plural people group
2) "The poor" = "Poor people" (plural)
3) Similar examples: the rich, the young, the elderly
4) Always use plural verb (have, are, were)

Memory Trick: "The poor" = "They" not "It"!

11

‚ö†Ô∏è Why tricky? Students want to add "The" before "my"!

2) Never: "the my" or "a my" - grammatically impossible
3) Without "my": "The teacher asked me..." ‚úì
4) One determiner rule: only one of (article/possessive/demonstrative)

üìñ Extra Study for Even Numbers Students

‚ö†Ô∏è Why tricky? Students forget musical instruments ALWAYS use "the"!

üéØ Advanced Analysis:

‚ö†Ô∏è Why tricky? Some students add "the" thinking it's a specific sport!

‚ö†Ô∏è Why tricky? Same word "school" but different meanings = different articles!
