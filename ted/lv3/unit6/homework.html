Exercise B - Vocabulary Matching

  • 1. constantly - all the time, continuously
  • 2. settle - to accept less than what you really want because it's easier
  • 3. overnight - in a single day, or over a very short period
  • 4. striking - immediately noticeable, usually in a positive or good sense
  • 5. deadline - a time by which something must be done
  • 6. wealth - a lot of money or things of great value
  • 7. dedicated - very committed to doing or achieving something
  • 8. crucial - very important
  • 9. bet - a choice to risk something in the hope of winning something better
  • 10. pursue - to try to obtain or achieve something, to chase something
