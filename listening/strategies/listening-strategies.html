The "Wrong Answer Hunt" Strategy

TEPS LISTENING STRATEGY LESSON

🎯 Strategy Overview

When choosing the correct response in TEPS listening conversations, eliminate answers that:

  • 🔁
    Repeat
    information already established
  • 🚗
    Change subject
    abruptly
  • ⚡
    Contradict
    the tone or direction
  • ⏪
    Go backwards
    in the conversation timeline
Remember: Choose the answer that naturally continues the conversation!

📝 Practice Examples

Question 14 - Correct Answer: (a)

W: I tried calling you this morning, but you didn't pick up the phone.
M: I was out of the office meeting a client until lunch.
W: Oh, I wasn't aware of that. Anyway, at least you're back now.

Analysis:

Last line: "Anyway, at least you're back now" → Shows W is ready to discuss something

Wrong answers eliminated:

(b) "Yeah, we had lunch at a really fancy place"
🚗 Changes subject - suddenly talks about lunch details
(c) "That's what I wanted to discuss with you"
⚡ Contradicts - M wasn't the one who called
(d) "When are you planning on calling me back?"
⏪ Goes backwards - they're already talking now!
✅ Why (a) is correct:
  • Naturally continues from "you're back now" → "let's talk"
  • Acknowledges W called for a reason
  • Moves the conversation forward

Question 15 - Correct Answer: (a)

M: How do you get most of your news these days? From the paper?
W: To be honest, I prefer reading news reports online. They're always more up-to-date.
M: Not me. I still like sitting down with a newspaper or watching the news on TV.

Analysis:

Last line: "I still like sitting down with a newspaper or watching the news on TV" → M prefers traditional media

Wrong answers eliminated:

(b) "I'm renewing my subscription to the local newspaper"
⚡ Contradicts - W just said she prefers online news!
(c) "Television news is definitely going to be big in the future"
🚗 Changes subject - talking about future, not preferences
⚡ Contradicts - ignores that TV news already exists
(d) "Anyway, I don't usually pay much attention to the news"
⚡ Contradicts - W just explained how she gets news
✅ Why (a) is correct:
  • Directly responds to M's preference for traditional media
  • Points out a disadvantage (old news)
  • Continues the comparison between online vs. traditional

📌 Quick Review Tips

  • 1 Read the last line carefully - it sets up what should come next
  • 2 Think "What would I say next?" in a real conversation
  • 3 Look for wrong answer patterns:
    🔁 = Already said that!
    🚗 = Random topic!
    ⚡ = That doesn't match!
    ⏪ = Old news!
  • 4 The correct answer always:
    • Responds to the last speaker
    • Moves the conversation forward
    • Makes logical sense
