Question 14

W: I tried calling you this morning, but you didn't pick up the phone.
M: I was out of the office meeting a client until lunch.
W: Oh, I wasn't aware of that. Anyway, at least you're back now.
(a) So, what do you want to talk to me about?
(b) Yeah, we had lunch at a really fancy place.
(c) That's what I wanted to discuss with you.
(d) When are you planning on calling me back?

Vocabulary

try~ing
~을 시도하다 (attempt to do something)
be aware of
~을 알다 (know about)
plan on ~ing
~할 예정이다, 계획이다 (intend to do)

Question 15

M: How do you get most of your news these days? From the paper?
W: To be honest, I prefer reading news reports online. They're always more up-to-date.
M: Not me. I still like sitting down with a newspaper or watching the news on TV.
(a) But so much of it is old by the time you hear it.
(b) I'm renewing my subscription to the local newspaper.
(c) Television news is definitely going to be big in the future.
(d) Anyway, I don't usually pay much attention to the news.

Vocabulary

up-to-date
최신의 (current, modern)
subscription
구독 (regular payment for service)
renew
갱신하다; 기한을 연장하다 (extend, continue)
pay attention to
~에 관심 갖다 (focus on, notice)
